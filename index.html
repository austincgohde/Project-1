The Green Elixir

Green tea, amazing hot or cold, just steep a tea bag or mix powder in to some hot water. But if you are like me you can steep a whole quart or more at one time and hold it cold and drink it as a refreshing cold healthy beverage (especially in 110 degree weather.) But. for some people that don't like the bitterness, it is incredibly simple to add many great flavors to your tea mixture. Simply, add some citrus in for some sweetness, ginger for extra health benefits and different flavor and many other great options.

Did you know?

  • Proven to help with cardiovascular disease.
  • Green tea lowers the risk of stroke.
  • 2nd most consumed beverage in the world behind water.
